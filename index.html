Types of Window Glasses

Tempered Glass

Strong and safe glass, designed to break into small harmless pieces.

Double Glazed

Two glass panes separated by air or gas for insulation and noise reduction.

Laminated Glass

Glass bonded with plastic layer for extra security and UV protection.

Low-E Coated Glass

Glass with special coating to reflect heat and improve energy efficiency.

Glass railings

No Reil

Glass railings can be installed with or without a top rail.

Top rail

A top rail shall not be required where the glass balusters are laminated glass with two or more glass plies of equal thickness and the same glass type when approved by the building official.

Handrail

handrails are either mounted on top of the glass panels (if a post system is used) or integrated directly into a frameless system.
